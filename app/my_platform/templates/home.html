Origin Of OwnIt

OwnIT originates from a FinTech Masters thesis, with the aim of revolutionizing digital provenance management. This solution establishes ownership rights, deters unauthorized copying, and protects intellectual property through advanced watermarking and cryptographic hash functions. With transparency at its core, users confidently claim ownership and trace asset provenance securely. This platform fosters collaboration among creators and rights holders, safeguarding the integrity of the digital ecosystem.

Our Values

We Believe In:

Integrity

By implementing robust watermarking algorithms and cryptographic hash functions, we ensure that digital provenance is maintained and protected.

Our platform enables users to establish a clear chain of ownership, discouraging illegal copying or unauthorized usage of copyrighted materials.

Collaboration

We value collaboration and recognize the importance of working together to combat digital piracy and protect intellectual property rights.
