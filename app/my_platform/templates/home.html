Origin Of OwnIt

OwnIT originates from a FinTech Masters thesis by Zipho Luvuno, with the aim of enhancing/automating digital provenance management. This solution establishes ownership rights, deters unauthorized copying, and protects intellectual property through advanced watermarking and cryptographic hash functions.

With transparency at its core, users confidently claim ownership and trace asset provenance securely. This platform fosters collaboration among creators and rights holders, safeguarding the integrity of the digital ecosystem.
